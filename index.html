About Apoorvaa

Apoorvaa is an engineer, with B.Tech in Computer Science from Netaji Subhash Institute of Technology, Delhi. During this time she indulged in lot of contests, hackathons and projects with her classmates. Following the studies, she joined a Hedge funds Company DE Shaw Ltd. in the software development field located in Hyderabad. Serving 1.5 years as a Member Technical she was promoted to Senior Member Technical. Currently she lives in Hyderabad, having spent more than 4.5 years in the firm.

About Bhavya

Bhavya is an engineer, with B.Tech in Computer Science from Netaji Subhash Institute of Technology, Delhi. Programming and development was his hobbies during the time, which led to creation of many projects. He joined as an Analyst in the Investment bank - Goldman Sachs. Later after promotion to Associate role, he switched his job as Software engineer in a Healthcare StartUp - H1. Currently he has been recruited as Senior Member Technical in DE Shaw Ltd.
